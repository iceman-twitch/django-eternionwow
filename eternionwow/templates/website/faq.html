  Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: Is Eternion-wow free to play?
A: Yes! Eternion-wow is completely FREE. All content is accessible without paying. Donations are optional and support server costs.

 

Q: What expansions do you support?
A: We currently support WotLK (3.3.5a) and Cataclysm (4.0.6) content across multiple realms. See our Realm Information for details.

 

Q: How many accounts can I have?
A: Players are limited to ONE account unless given special permission by administration. Multiple accounts may result in ban.

 

Q: Can I transfer my character from another server?
A: Character transfers are not currently supported. You must start fresh on Eternion-wow.

 

Getting Started

Q: How do I start playing?
A: Follow these steps:
1. Register an account
2. Download the appropriate WoW client from our Downloads page
3. Change your realmlist (instructions on Connect page)
4. Login and create your character!

 

Q: What is a realmlist?
A: The realmlist tells your WoW client which server to connect to. You must change it to point to Eternion-wow. See our Connect Guide.

 

Q: I can't connect to the server. What should I do?
A: Check these common issues:
 Verify your realmlist.wtf file is correct
 Make sure you're using the correct WoW client version
 Check if the realm is online (see homepage status)
 Disable firewall/antivirus temporarily
 Try flushing DNS: ipconfig /flushdns

 

Account Issues

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Use the "Retrieve Password" feature in the Account Tools section (left sidebar when logged in) or contact us via Contact page.

 

Q: My character is stuck. What can I do?
A: Use the "Unstuck Character" feature in Account Tools (requires login). This will teleport your character to a safe location.

 

Q: Can I change my account email?
A: Yes, contact a GM via ticket in-game or use our Contact page for email changes.

 

Gameplay

Q: What are the XP rates?
A: XP rates vary by realm:
 Eternion80 3.3.x: x5 rates
 Apocalypse Fun 3.3.x: x255 instant rates
 Deathwing 4.0.X: x10 rates
 Hellscream Cataclysm 4.0.X: x15 rates

 

Q: Are cross-faction interactions allowed?
A: Cross-faction chat, mail, and grouping are disabled on PvP realms to maintain faction balance. PvE realms may have cross-faction features enabled.

 

Q: Do you have working raids and dungeons?
A: Yes! All major raids and dungeons are scripted and working. Some minor bugs may exist - report them via ticket.

 

Voting & Donations

Q: How does voting work?
A: Vote for Eternion-wow on server listing sites to earn Vote Points. Redeem points for rewards on our Vote Rewards page. You can vote every 12 hours.

 

Q: Are donations required to play?
A: No! All content is accessible for free. Donations are optional and help keep the server running. Donors receive cosmetic items and convenience features.

 

Q: Can I get a refund on my donation?
A: No. All donations are final and non-refundable. See our Terms of Use for details.

 

Technical Support

Q: My game crashes frequently. What can I do?
A: Try these solutions:
 Update your graphics drivers
 Lower in-game graphics settings
 Disable addons temporarily
 Delete Cache, WDB, and WTF folders (backup first!)
 Run WoW.exe as administrator

 

Q: Where can I get help if my question isn't answered here?
A: You can:
 Submit a ticket in-game (type .ticket in chat)
 Ask in our Forums
 Contact Us directly
